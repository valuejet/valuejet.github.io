The terms and conditions of your e-ticket varies according to the fare basis applicable to base fare paid. The different booking classes are grouped in fare families with different penalties and fees applicable.

Passengers are advised to cancel their unwanted confirmed flight reservations at least three (3) hours before the departure of the scheduled flight. Failing to do so will incur a no-show penalty of 25,000 naira. Alternatively, confirmed flight reservations may be cancelled for use at a later date.

Passengers are advised that some fare bases will incur a refund penalty of 4000 naira, while others allow for refunds without penalty or are non-refundable. Passengers are advised that some fare bases will incur a date change penalty of 3000 naira, while others allow for free date change.

The Premium class Passengers are advised that the tickets are non-transferable, valid on VK flights on, NGN5000 no-show fee applies, free date change; and the tickets are fully refundable.

Check-in counters open three (3) hours before the scheduled departure of the flight and closes 30 minutes before the departure.

Note: All Lagos flight departures for Premium passengers depart from the new MMA2 Premium departure lounge situated at the right-hand side of the MMA2 departure terminal.
